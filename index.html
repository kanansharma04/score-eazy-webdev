How do I book my first counseling session?

Booking your first session is easy! Click the "Book Session" button anywhere on our site, choose your preferred counselor and time slot, and complete the brief intake form. We'll send you a confirmation email with all the details.

What should I expect in my first session?

Your first session is all about getting to know you. We'll discuss your goals, challenges, and what you hope to achieve. There's no pressure - it's a safe space to share as much or as little as you're comfortable with.

Are the sessions confidential?

Absolutely. All our sessions are completely confidential and follow professional counseling ethics guidelines. Your privacy and trust are our top priorities.

Can I switch counselors if needed?

Of course! Finding the right fit is important for your success. If you'd like to try working with a different counselor, just let us know and we'll help you find someone who better matches your needs.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes! We offer both in-person and online sessions to fit your schedule and comfort level. Our secure video platform ensures your privacy while providing the same quality support.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

We understand that life happens! You can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your session without any fees. Less than 24 hours notice may incur a cancellation fee.
